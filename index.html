KIRA COMMANDS

#help

support you to get knowledge about all commands

#play

Plays a song with the given name or url.

#volume

set volume output as you want.

#now playing

Shows what song kira is currently playing

#stop

To stop current playing song.

#queue

To check how many song in queue

#skip

skip the current playing song.

#lyrics

Gets the lyrics of the current playing song.

#pause

pause playing song.

#resume

Resumes paused music.

#stats

show systeam streaming status.

#meme

watch funny memes by r/memes.

#loop

Toggles looping for the current playing song.

#connect

active the bot to the voice channel.

#disconnect

Disconnects the bot from the voice channel.

#uptime

check online analysis of kira.
